The problem

30 to 40% of fashion is produced but never sold.

Every drop leaves €30k-100k unsold, and its value falls week by week. The three legacy exits all lose money or damage the brand:

  • Exit ADiscount at homeCannibalises full-price sales on your own channels.
  • Exit BDump to off-pricePublic markdowns, right next to your full-price stock.
  • Exit CThe grey marketSub-€1 jobber lots that destroy brand equity.

We are building the fourth exit.

A new deadline

From July 19, 2026, destroying unsold textiles becomes illegal.

Under EU Regulation 2024/1781 (ESPR), large firms must find a brand-safe exit for unsold apparel. Sub-premium brands follow in 2030. Every brand in Europe needs a new channel.
