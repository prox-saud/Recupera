    How it works

    One transaction, three steps. We never hold inventory.

    1. 01

      List

      The brand sends its stock file. We build the listing with pricing and visibility rules set to the brand's preferences. Listings stay anonymous until the brand approves a buyer.

    2. 02

      Match

      Vetted boutiques request lots that fit their market and season. We confirm both sides before any introduction, and we handle the invoice.

    3. 03

      Ship direct

      We handle matching and invoicing. The brand ships direct from its own warehouse. Fulfillment support is available as an optional add-on for brands without export capability.
